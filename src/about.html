Nice to meet you!

I’m Nina Le (She/Her), a third-year design student at York University and a UX/UI designer pursuing an Honours Bachelor of Design. I’m passionate about creating thoughtful, inclusive solutions that optimize feasibility, viability, and desirability for current or future features. Whether working on UX/UI or experimental projects, I love diving into research and collaborating with cross-functional teams to craft experiences that truly cater to the user.

My attention to detail—shaped by years practicing music and illustration—fuels my approach to design. Combined with my industry experience, this foundation drives my focus on accessibility, precision, and creating work that feels impactful.

My Design Values

Responsible design

Applying human-centered design principles to decision-making. Ensuring solutions are ethical, inclusive, and considerate of their impact on all stakeholders.

Play where needed

Crafting designs that balance delight and usability. Aligning with trends without compromising the functionality or user flow.

Precision and infrastructure

Combining micro-interactions, hierarchy, and attention to detail so experiences feel effortless yet intentional, while focusing on your iterations to ensure designs are grounded from research and user feedback to deliver the best results. Prioritizing accessibility, equity, and sustainability while aligning with broader business goals.
